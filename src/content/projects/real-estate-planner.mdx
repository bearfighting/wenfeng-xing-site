Real Estate Planner sits between financial modeling and product design.

The real challenge is not just the math. It is presenting competing assumptions clearly enough that a person can actually reason through them.

## Current direction

I am treating this as a research-heavy product:

- the modeling needs to be correct
- the assumptions need to stay visible
- the output needs to help decisions, not overwhelm them
